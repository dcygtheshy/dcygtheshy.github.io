This is the first note on my new personal site.

这里会逐步沉淀我的研究札记、工程笔记、保研经历和一些更松弛的思考。短期目标是让它成为一个公开、稳定、好看的知识入口；长期目标是让每篇笔记都能连接到更大的问题。

## Why This Site Exists

- To keep research notes close to the projects and choices that produced them.
- To write in both Chinese and English without forcing every idea into one audience.
- To make the work visible enough that future collaborators can understand the direction quickly.
